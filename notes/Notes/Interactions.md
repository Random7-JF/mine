
Hotbar will have "Modes"
1. Track mode
2. Weapon mode
3. Interact mode

## Track Mode
- You will be able to place or remove tracks.
- removing tracks will add to your inventory
- you have a limited amount of tracks to place
- selecting a track that is already there could be used to rotate it

## Weapon Mode
- you  will have a weapon you can swing or use to attack enemies

## Interact Mode
- you will use this to be able to interact with switches or chests
- pick up items off the ground
